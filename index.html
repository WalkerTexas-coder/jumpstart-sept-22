Ingredients

  • Large Granny Smith Apples
  • Large Honeycrisp Apples
  • Large Golden Delicious Apples
  • Brown Sugar
  • Pie Crust
  • Cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Mix ingredients together
  3. Pour ingredients into pie crust
  4. Bake for 25 minutes
